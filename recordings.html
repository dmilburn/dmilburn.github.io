Recordings



Tzigane by Maurice Ravel

Tzigane means "Gypsy". This piece was inspired by gypsy music although it was written by a French composer.



String Quartet op 44 no. 3 by Felix Mendelssohn

My favorite part of being in a string quartet was learning to work so closely in a team. Chamber music is incredibly fun and difficult.

Mendelssohn was a composer during the romantic period. However, his music tends to be more reserved an refined than a lot of other romantic composers. He tends to pull a lot of techniques from earlier eras, such as fugues.



Chaconne by Johann Sebastian Bach

This is my all time favorite piece by my all time favorite composer.

Bach was a genius. This entire 13 minute piece has the same bassline the entire time and somehow is constantly interesting and evolving.
